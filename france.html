Best of France

Crepes

A crepe is a very thin pancake, that can be eaten for breakfast, or as a dessert. They can be either sweet or savory.

Escargot

Escargot is a cooked snail, often served as an appetizer.

Macarons

Macarons are a sweet, meringue like food that can be eaten as a dessert, or a sweet snack.

Croissants

Croissants are a buttery, flakey pastry that is often eaten as breakfast.

Quiche

Quiche is a savory dish, that can be served either hot or cold, and is often a party dish.

Steak Tartare

Steak Tartare is a dish made up of raw ground beef, usually served with a raw egg on top.

Coq au Vin

Coq au vin is chicken braised with wine, mushrooms and lardon.

Cassoulet

Cassoulet is a slow cooked cassarole, usually with meat, pork skin and beans.

Places to go in France!

In order to really explore France, there are many monuments and places to explore.

The first place that you should go is the Eiffel Tower. This is an iconic monument that you can climb to the top of and are able to see a view of Paris.

Another place you need to go is The Louvre. The Louvre is an art museum in Paris, that has many amazing paintings including the Mona Lisa.

The Normandy beaches are a beautiul place to go. This is where D-Day took place during World War II.

There are many other amazing places to visit in France including Arc de Triumphe, Mont Saint-Michel, Palace of Versailles and many more.
